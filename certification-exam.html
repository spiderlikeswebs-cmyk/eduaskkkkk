Certification Overview

EduAsk Professional Certifications are designed to evaluate conceptual understanding and real-world knowledge in modern technology and business domains.

These certifications help students:

  • Validate their knowledge
  • Test their analytical thinking
  • Demonstrate practical learning
  • Build confidence in technical subjects

The certification system focuses on conceptual depth and real understanding rather than memorization.

Examination Details

  • Certification Type EduAsk Professional Certification
  • Test Format Multiple Choice & Logical Reasoning
  • Total Questions 40
  • Duration 60 Minutes
  • Difficulty Level Advanced
  • Passing Marks 85%
  • Result Displayed immediately
  • Award Digital Certificate

High Difficulty Certification

The EduAsk certification examination is designed to evaluate deep conceptual understanding and problem-solving ability.

This is not a basic test. The questions focus on analytical reasoning and real knowledge.

Only candidates scoring 85 percent or above will successfully receive the EduAsk Professional Certificate.

Topics Covered

The examination evaluates a wide range of core professional competencies.

Data Structures & Algorithms

Evaluation of core algorithmic optimization and structuring knowledge.

Programming Logic

Testing fundamental coding concepts, loops, recursion, and object-oriented principles.

Artificial Intelligence Concepts

Understanding models, machine learning lifecycle, and AI application design.

Cloud Technology Fundamentals

Evaluating knowledge of basic infrastructure as a service, deployments, and distributed systems.

Business Data Interpretation

Testing skills in analyzing trends, statistics, and business intelligence reporting.

Digital Computing Skills

Assessing essential literacy across software, networking, and digital productivity tools.
