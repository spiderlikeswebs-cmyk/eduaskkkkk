General Questions

What is EduASK?

EduASK is a premium technical certification platform designed to help students and professionals bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world proficiency. We offer industry-recognized certifications across various domains including Web Development, Cloud Computing, and Data Science.

Are the certifications globally recognized?

Yes, EduASK Certificators is a global benchmark for technical mastery. Our certificates are widely accepted by leading tech companies and can be verified online through our dedicated verification portal using a unique certificate ID.

How do I start a course?

Simply navigate to our Certifications page, select the course that interests you, and click "Get Certified". You will be guided through the syllabus and examination process immediately.

What is the Educational Consultancy service?

Our Educational Consultancy helps students find the perfect online degree program in India. We partner with over 50 top universities to provide expert guidance on admissions, eligibility, and career outcomes.

Is there a fee for the certificates?

Courses and standard examinations are accessible to all learners. A nominal fee may apply for high-level specialized certifications to cover the cost of proctoring and official verification services.
